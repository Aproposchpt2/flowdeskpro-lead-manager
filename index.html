Correct Flow

The dashboard receives records. It does not originate the purchase.

External product pages, signup intake forms, calls, SMS, and appointment requests create records through Netlify Functions. The dashboard is the place your staff reviews, prioritizes, updates, and follows up.

Product selection

Customers choose a website package or FlowDesk Pro product on the AI4 Product Purchasing site.

Record creation

The source site posts customer and product metadata to the Lead Manager submit endpoint.

Command center

Your team sees the record, payment/source metadata, selected product, selected plan, and next action.

Supported Sources

Built for external lead channels.

Purchase site

Web intake
