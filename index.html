Product explanation

One place to see what needs attention.

The Lead Manager Dashboard gives businesses a cleaner way to review customer-response activity. It is designed to help teams see what came in, what needs attention, and what should happen next.

Review captured activity

Review calls, intake requests, names, contact details, reasons for contact, and record summaries.

Filter and prioritize

Use urgency, status, and source context to focus on the right follow-up first.

Operational visibility

Reduce scattered follow-up by turning requests into visible records.
